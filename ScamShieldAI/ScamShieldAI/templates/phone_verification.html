Phone Scam Warning Signs
  • Urgency tactics: Pressure to act immediately
  • Personal information requests: Asking for SSN, passwords, or account details
  • Money requests: Asking for payments, gift cards, or wire transfers
  • Robocalls: Automated messages with suspicious offers
  • Spoofed numbers: Calls appearing to come from legitimate organizations
Protection Tips
  • Never give personal information over the phone unless you initiated the call
  • Verify caller identity by calling the organization directly
  • Register your number with the Do Not Call Registry
  • Use call-blocking apps or features
  • Report suspicious calls to authorities
